Programs and Events

GreenSprout offers educational programs and seasonal events that support youth learning, adult gardening skills, and community participation.

Youth Garden Classes

Hands-on activities that teach students about seeds, soil, nutrition, and environmental responsibility.

Adult Workshops

Practical classes on container gardening, composting, seasonal planting, and garden maintenance.

School Partnerships

Partnerships that help teachers connect classroom lessons with outdoor learning experiences.
