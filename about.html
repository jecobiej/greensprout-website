About GreenSprout

GreenSprout Community Garden is a fictional neighborhood organization created to promote gardening, healthy food access, environmental education, and community involvement. The website plan was based on the Unit III assignment, which described the organization as a new site designed for local families, schools, community partners, volunteers, sponsors, and residents interested in fresh food and environmental learning.

Our mission is to make gardening approachable for all residents, whether they are experienced growers or first-time participants. We believe community gardens strengthen neighborhoods by creating shared spaces where people can learn, serve, and build relationships.

Healthy Food Access

We encourage residents to grow fresh produce and participate in seasonal harvest events.

Environmental Learning

Our workshops teach composting, soil care, water conservation, pollinator support, and sustainable practices.

Neighborhood Involvement

Volunteer days and community events help residents connect while improving shared garden spaces.
